Welcome

Every year, a group of KNU, PNU, OMU, and OU topologists and their academic families and friends is organizing an international conference on knots and related topics alternately at Kyungpook National University, Pusan National University, and Osaka Metropolitan University. The 16th TAPU-KOOK Joint Seminar on Knots and Related Topics and the 18th Graduate Student Workshop on Mathematics will take place at the Department of Mathematics, Kyungpook National University, July 29-31, 2025.
